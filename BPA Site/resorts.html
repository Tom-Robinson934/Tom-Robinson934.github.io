Resorts

Where to stay?

The official conference properties

    

Disney Yacht Club Resort

1700 Epcot Resorts Boulevard Lake Buena Vista, Florida 32830-8407

 

    Disney's Yacht Club is a beautiful lakeside hotel with various recreational areas and dining. You can enjoy a refreshing swim at one of many pools. You can show off your skills on the tennis court or volleyball court. There are a variety of video games that can be played at the arcade. Fairways Miniature Golf Course is exciting for those looking for a challenge.

 

    You won't go hungry in Disney's Yacht Club! Savor a breakfast, lunch and dinner at various locations around the resort. The Market at Ale & Compass provides dining and shopping. If you aren't looking for a huge meal the Ale & Compass Lounge is for you. Around dinner time the Ale & Compass Restaurant makes seafood dishes that everyone can enjoy.

 

    

Disney Coronado Springs Resort

1000 West Buena Vista Drive Lake Buena Vista, Florida 32830-8403

 

    Disney Coronado Springs is a stunning lakeside oasis that offers activities and eateries. Three Bridges Bar and Grill at Villa del Lago is a great place to get unwind and look at the beautiful view. At the pool there is a giant 50 foot replica of a mayan pyramid. Water streaming down the side forms the spitting jaguar slide. As the sun goes down, gather round the crackling campfire to roast marshmallows. Watch beloved Disney films at outdoor screenings.

 

Dont miss your stay!
